# Safety and Ethics

This project is defensive and educational. It does not include real patient data, real protected health information, live system access, credentials, malware, exploit code, or instructions for compromising systems.

## Data rules

- Use synthetic referral examples only.
- Do not use real patient names, dates of birth, addresses, MRNs, SSNs, insurance IDs, or clinical records.
- Do not connect the project to live healthcare systems.
- Do not test against systems without permission.

## Responsible scope

The project studies whether an AI assistant can resist unsafe instructions in a controlled environment. The goal is to improve secure design, risk awareness, and human-review workflows.

## Human review

The assistant should escalate clinical, payer, legal, privacy, and high-risk workflow ambiguity to qualified human reviewers.
