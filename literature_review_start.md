# Literature Review Starter

## OWASP LLM Top 10

OWASP identifies prompt injection as a top risk for LLM applications. The risk is relevant here because the assistant receives untrusted referral text and may confuse data with instructions.

Source: https://genai.owasp.org/llmrisk/llm01-prompt-injection/

## NIST AI Risk Management Framework: Generative AI Profile

NIST's Generative AI Profile extends the AI Risk Management Framework with risks and management actions specific to generative AI systems. This project maps naturally to the RMF functions: Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage.

Source: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

## Anthropic Fellows Program: AI Security

Anthropic's AI Security Fellows track focuses on empirical research and security risks from advanced AI systems. The official posting emphasizes Python fluency, full-time availability, clear technical thinking, implementation speed, and motivation to make AI safe and beneficial.

Source: https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/anthropic/jobs/5030244008
